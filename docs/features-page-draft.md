## Detailed Features

### 1. Smart Paste (AI-Powered)
- **Problem**: Resellers waste hours copying and pasting details manually from WhatsApp chats to notes or spreadsheets.
- **Solution**: Paste the entire chat message into ResellerPro. Our AI automatically extracts the customer's name, phone number, delivery address, and even email.
- **How it works**:
    1. Copy customer details from WhatsApp/Instagram.
    2. Click 'Smart Paste' in the ResellerPro Dashboard.
    3. Review and Save instantly.

### 2. Centralized CRM Database
- **Details**: Store unlimited customer profiles with high-level organization.
- **Features**: Track order history, save personalized notes, tag customers for easy filtering, and see lifetime value.
- **Benefit**: Never lose a customer's contact or order history again.

### 3. Order Management System
- **Details**: A dedicated workflow for tracking sales from inquiry to delivery.
- **Status Tracking**: Pending -> Shipped -> Delivered.
- **Profit Calculation**: Auto-calculate profit for every order based on cost and selling price.

### 4. WhatsApp & Instagram Workflow
- **Details**: Specifically designed for the "Social Commerce" way of selling.
- **Features**: Follow-up reminders, lead tracking, and quick-reply templates.

### 5. Real-Time Analytics
- **Details**: A birds-eye view of your business health.
- **Metrics**: Revenue, Profit, Top Products, and Customer Growth trends.

### 6. Enterprise-Grade Security
- **Details**: Data encryption and privacy-first design ensures your business secrets and customer data are safe.
